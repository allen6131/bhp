Apartments

Gracious living at a great price

The apartments at Brook Highland Place have been recently renovated for a modern, open lifestyle. Our spacious garden style units are larger than comparable apartments in the area, so you get more living space for your money. They feature wood burning fireplaces, new flooring throughout, and tons of closet space and storage. Each unit has a private balcony, most with expansive lake views. Our “open concept” kitchens are fitted with new cabinets, Caesarstone™ countertops, and modern stainless steel appliances. Most units are ready for your washer/dryer. There are 1, 2 and 3 bedroom layouts with 7 great floor plans to choose from.

Resort-like landscaping Brook Highland Place is neatly arranged on 20+ acres of sculpted landscape. There are just under 400 units in 25 separate garden-style buildings wrapped around tranquil Brook Highland Lake, only minutes away from Oak Mountain State Park. The grounds are quiet, private, and a tranquil place to call home. You’ll share fun times at our 2 well-kept swimming pools, club house and children’s playground. There’s even a large fenced park for dogs to run free.
